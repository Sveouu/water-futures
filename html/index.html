What is Water Futures?

This portal gives you access to models developed by researchers out of the University of Oxford. The goal is to give more people access to the tools used by scientists and authorities to manage environmental challenges.

WATER FUTURES provides an easy-to-use interface for exploring how changes in climate and other environmental conditions impact the way water is supplied to your home, school and places of work.

Objectives

The goal of WATER FUTURES is to visualise potential futures for water availability, scarcity, and mobility across East London
