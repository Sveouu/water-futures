WATER FUTURES: EAST LONDON

The following maps were developed by a team of researchers at the University of Oxford to help you visualise how changes in climate and other environmental conditions can impact how water reaches your home, school and places of work.

Click EXPLORE in the menu above to imagine different scenarios of water moving around East London now and in the future.

Water Futures: East London is a collaborative effort between the University of Oxford's MaRIUS project and Thames21.
