Turning on the tap

This homepage should serve as an introduction to the project and the tool. There is opportunity to include images, video clips, even maps and charts which compose to make the visualisation tool.

The main idea is to remember some details of water supply, to remind the casual visitor that there is a complicated system behind the apparently simple action of turning on a tap and seeing clean water pour out.
